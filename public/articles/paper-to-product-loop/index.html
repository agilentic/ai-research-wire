From Paper to Product: The Agentic Research Loop

The core job of an AI research publication is not only to summarize papers. It is to preserve the path from claim to implementation.

A good research note includes

  • The paper's central claim.
  • The exact experimental setup.
  • What would need to be true for the claim to matter commercially.
  • The simplest reproduction path.
  • Open questions and failure modes.
  • Product ideas unlocked by the result.

Why Markdown works

Markdown keeps the research artifact portable. A note can start as a Telegram request, become a Hermes-generated draft, get reviewed in GitHub, and publish as a static article without being trapped in a CMS.
